Confidential Business Report // 2026

1. Project Overview

Two students, Maya and Alex, are launching a weekend smoothie stall called "Berry Blast." They plan to sell two specific types of premium smoothies: the "Sunrise Zing" and the "Green Machine."

They have secured a loan of $100.00 from their parents to buy ingredients. Their goal is to pay back the loan and make a profit of at least $50.00 in the first week. To do this, they must calculate their costs very carefully using the "Bulk Supply" price list.
