Case Study Answer Key

Q1: Unit Prices

Strawberries: $12.50 ÷ 2.5 = $5.00 / kg
Spinach: $6.40 ÷ 0.8 = $8.00 / kg
Orange Juice: $4.50 ÷ 1.5 = $3.00 / Liter
Coconut Water: $8.00 ÷ 2.0 = $4.00 / Liter

Q2: Batch Cost (Sunrise Zing)

Recipe A Ingredients:

Strawberries: 0.5 kg × $5.00 = $2.50
Orange Juice: 0.2 L × $3.00 = $0.60
Banana: 1 × $0.45 = $0.45
---------------------------
Total Batch Cost = $3.55

Q3: Cost Per Cup

Batch makes 4 cups.

$3.55 ÷ 4 = $0.8875

(Rounding: Since this is money, she might round to $0.89. However, for internal accounting, keeping precise decimals is better. Let's assume she uses $0.89).

Q4: Revenue & Profit (Sunrise Zing)

From Chart: 32 Cups sold.

A) Revenue: 32 cups × $3.50 = $112.00
B) Total Cost: 32 cups × $0.89 = $28.48
C) Profit: $112.00 - $28.48 = $83.52

Q5: The Green Machine Strategy (Hard!)

Goal: Make $83.52 profit on Green Machines.

Data: 24 Cups sold. Cost is $1.40/cup.

1. Total Cost for 24 cups: 24 × $1.40 = $33.60
2. Required Revenue: Total Cost + Target Profit
$33.60 + $83.52 = $117.12
3. Price Per Cup: Revenue ÷ Cups
$117.12 ÷ 24 = $4.88
Answer: They must charge $4.88 per cup.
